STELLAR ORACLE

星空之谕 · Tarot Divination

Before the first temple was raised, before the first prayer was whispered into the dark — there were the stars. Unmoving in their courses, yet tracing lines of meaning across the vault of night, they have been the silent companions of every human question ever asked.

在第一座神殿建起之前，在第一句祷词没入黑暗之前——星辰便已在。它们在轨迹中不动，却在夜的穹顶划出意义的线条，是每一个人类曾问过的问题的无声伴侣。

The ancients understood what we, in our haste, have forgotten: that the outer sky and the inner soul are woven from the same thread. To gaze upward is to gaze inward. The cards do not invent your story — they remember it, piece by piece, from a place in you that has always known.

古人懂得我们在匆忙中已然遗忘的事：外部的天空与内在的灵魂，是由同一根线织成的。仰望星空，即是凝视内心。牌并不发明你的故事——它们记起它，一片一片地，从你心中一向知晓的所在。

Some days call for a single word — a quiet companion to carry through the hours. Other days demand a fuller conversation, a deeper reckoning with the forces that shape your path. Wherever you stand, the cards are ready. The question is not whether the stars speak, but whether you are still enough to listen.

有些日子只需要一个字——一位安静的同伴陪你度过时光。有些日子则需要一场更深的对话，与塑造你道路的力量进行一次更完整的清算。无论你站在何处，牌已备好。问题不在于星辰是否言语，而在于你是否足够安静去聆听。

Daily Whisper · 星辰低语

A single card, a single message — what do the stars whisper to you today?

一张牌，一句话——今天星辰对你低语了什么？

The ancients drew a single lot at dawn — not to command the day, but to learn which force walked beside them. One card. One whisper. One thread pulled from the great weave. The stars do not shout; they lean close and murmur. In the space of a single image, a single symbol, the whole sky bends near. You are not being told what will happen — you are being shown what already stirs within you, waiting to be known.

· · ·

古人于黎明抽一支签——不为掌控这一日，只为知晓今日与何种力量同行。一张牌。一声低语。从伟大织锦中抽出的一根线。星辰从不嘶吼，它们俯身低语。在一幅图像、一个符号的空间里，整片天空弯下腰来靠近你。你被告知的并非将要发生什么——你被展示的，是早已在你心中涌动、等待被你认出的事物。
